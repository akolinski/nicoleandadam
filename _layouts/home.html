Useful info

We put this section together to help you with your stay in Cronulla.

Uber

Get an Uber to get around in Cronulla. Download Uber from the app store or android store if you have a smart phone. If not you can request a ride from their website by signing up.

Train

Cronulla is the last station on the Cronulla line. It is a 50 minute journey into Sydney central and a 45 minute train from Sydney Airport. If you plan to use the train we recommend you get an opal card. The card works just like an oyster/myki card and can be purchased from train stations and news agencies. You can also purchase a single ticket at train stations if need be. Wanda Surf club is a 20 minute walk from Cronulla train station or a 5 minute Uber ride.
